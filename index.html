Exploring the Ancient Trade Routes and Cultural Heritage

Introduction

The Silk Road in China was a vast and dynamic network of trade routes that connected the East and West, fostering economic, cultural, and religious exchanges for centuries. This website offers an interactive map of the Silk Road's main routes in China, in-depth profiles of five key heritage sites, a historical timeline, and insights into the cultural legacy of this ancient network. Explore the journey that shaped civilizations and discover the enduring impact of the Silk Road.
